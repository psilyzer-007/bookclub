This is your margin.

The best part of any well-loved book isn't the text — it's the marginalia. The underlines, the dog-ears, the notes scrawled at 1 AM. They tell you more about a reader than anything else could.

What is Project Marginalia?

Part library catalogue. Part yearbook. Part "stalking your TBR respectfully."

Project Marginalia is our effort to collect yours — a place where members can discover each other's current reads, Substack / Goodreads / Fable / StoryGraph profiles, reading goals, favourite recommendations, and connect beyond just "Hi" in the WhatsApp group.
