Who We Are

Readers finding each other across Odisha

The Book Club of Communitie Odisha brings together readers from Bhubaneswar, Cuttack, and beyond — united by a love for books, stories, and the conversations they spark.

The ବହି Registry is our living member directory — a place to discover who's reading what, find your next book recommendation, or connect with someone who shares your taste for Russian literature or cozy fantasy.

ବହି (pronounced "Bohi") means book in Odia.
